# Welcome to Aurelius Ledger

Aurelius Ledger is your trading companion. It helps you track trades, monitor your mental state, and catch bad habits before they cost you money.

This guide walks you through everything you need to get the most out of your sessions.

## What Aurelius Ledger Does

- **Logs your trades** from natural language — just type what happened
- **Scores your discipline and agency** — are you trading your plan or reacting emotionally?
- **Watches for tilt** — catches revenge trading, fear, and frustration patterns
- **Shows you trends** — see how your psychology evolves throughout the session

## Who This Is For

You trade futures — NQ, ES, RTY, or CL. You've got a strategy, but sometimes emotions get the better of you. You want to get better at staying disciplined and making decisions with intention, not reaction.

If that sounds like you, Aurelius Ledger is built for you.

## How It Works

1. **Log a trade** — Type what happened in plain English
2. **The AI reads your description** — It figures out direction, outcome, P&L, and how disciplined you were
3. **You see the results** — Charts update instantly showing your P&L, discipline, and agency over time
4. **The coach chimes in** — After enough trades, AI insights start appearing

It is that simple. No forms to fill out, no dropdowns to manage. Just type and move on with your session.

## The Psychology Stuff

Aurelius Ledger tracks two scores:

- **Discipline** — Did you wait for your setup? Did you follow your rules?
- **Agency** — Are you in control of your decisions, or are you reacting to the market?

Both scores range from -1 to +1. The system watches for patterns — like taking trades after a loss to "make it back" — and gives you a heads up before things get worse.

:::tip This Is Not About Perfection
You will not score +1 every trade. Nobody does. The point is awareness. The more you see your patterns, the more you can work on them.
:::
